Internet of Things (IoT)

The Internet of Things connects everyday devices to the internet, enabling them to send, receive, and act on data. Smart homes, wearable devices, and connected industries are examples of IoT in action.

Why IoT Matters

IoT allows for real-time monitoring, improved efficiency, and automation. It transforms how we interact with technology, from energy management to logistics and healthcare.

Real-World Applications

  • Smart home automation and connected appliances.
  • Industrial IoT for predictive maintenance.
  • Connected healthcare devices for remote monitoring.
  • Smart cities and transportation systems.

By connecting devices intelligently, IoT provides insights and efficiencies that were previously impossible, creating smarter environments everywhere.
